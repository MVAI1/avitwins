Our Values

With the AVI-TWINS, poultry farm administrators can monitor farm conditions in real time. Key features of our system include:

User Friendly Dashboard

Real-time farm monitoring enables the detection and alerting of anomalies in health and growth, as well as the monitoring of the farm's ecological footprint. This system facilitates instant and efficient intervention, preventing potential issues before they escalate.

Advanced anomaly detection using multimodal AI algorithms

Through multimodal AI algorithms utilizing images, voice, and natural language processing, we can detect anomalies and identify patterns related to growth and health. This enables us to propose immediate mitigation plans, ensuring timely and effective interventions.

Farm Digital twins representation

Farm digital twins, created using IoT and photogrammetry, play a crucial role in predictive maintenance by monitoring equipment performance to prevent breakdowns. They minimize disease transmission by reducing physical interaction with livestock and enable remote verification, auditing, and certification to ensure regulatory compliance.

Support( 24/7) with Advanced Poultry chatbot

The oriented poultry chatbot provides reliable answers to solve problems encountered on your farm. It offers precise recommendations for actions to remedy anomalies and connects you with experts for further assistance.

Contextualized Biomonitoring

By monitoring biosecurity practices, AVI-TWINS enhances biosecurity readiness and epidemiological awareness through contextualized biomonitoring.

Smart Safe and Sustainable Label

Each farm will receive a 'Smart, Safe, and Sustainable' label by production cycle, a digital certification securely kept on the blockchain. This certification allows farmers to demonstrate product quality and compliance with regulations and standards.
